# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2025  
**Owner:** Ayman Seif, Elmahrosa International (Est. 2007)

### Data Controller

Elmahrosa International  
Represented by: Ayman Seif  
Jurisdiction: Alexandria, Egypt

### Data We Collect

#### From Pi Network
- Pi Network User ID
- Username
- Wallet address (for treasury operations)

#### From Platform Usage
- Badge assignments
- Petition signatures
- Treasury contributions
- Civic share certificates

### How We Use Data

1. **Governance:** Track petition signatures and voting
2. **Treasury:** Record contributions and issue certificates
3. **Badge System:** Manage access and permissions
4. **Auditing:** Enable transparency and compliance
5. **Communication:** Project updates to pioneers

### Data Sharing

We do NOT share data with third parties except:

- **Pi Network:** Transaction verification
- **Auditors:** Compliance and oversight
- **Legal:** If required by Egyptian law

### Data Retention

- **Active Pioneers:** Data retained indefinitely
- **Inactive Pioneers:** Data retained for 7 years
- **Treasury Records:** Permanent (blockchain)
- **Incident Logs:** Permanent (transparency)

### Your Rights

- **Access:** Request copy of your data
- **Correction:** Update incorrect information
- **Deletion:** Request removal (except blockchain records)
- **Portability:** Receive data in standard format
- **Objection:** Opt out of non-essential processing

### Security Measures

- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Badge-gated access controls
- Regular security audits
- Incident response procedures

### International Transfers

Data is stored in Egypt and may be accessed internationally by approved badge holders under NDA.

### Cookies and Tracking

- Essential cookies for authentication only
- No third-party analytics or tracking
- Pi Network SDK cookies per their policy

### Children's Privacy

Platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

### Changes to Policy

- Governed by petition system
- 30-day notice for major changes
- Continued use implies acceptance

### Contact

For privacy inquiries:

- Subject to NDA and verification
